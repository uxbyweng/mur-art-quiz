The Street Artist Quiz

Quiz: Do you know the street artists?

Question #01

Which street artists created this beautiful mural in Berlin!

Herakut Victor Ash Invader ROA Dave the Chimp Tank
#astronaut #mural #kreuzberg

Answer #01

Victor Ash

This giant Spaceman mural called "Astronaut/Cosmonaut" was created by the French artist Victor Ash in 2007 in Berlin’s Kreuzberg. The floating astronaut points to the Cold War and space race, linking back to the city’s divided past. Today it’s one of Berlin’s most famous street art landmarks.

Answer #02

This mural "My home might be no palace, but we can share it if you like." was created by the artistic duo Herakut, finished May 2018. It is located near the Moritzplatz in Berlin Kreuzberg.
